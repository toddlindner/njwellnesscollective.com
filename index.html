NJ WELLNESS COLLECTIVE

Welcome

Give yourself or someone else the gift of wellness as you shop this collection of practitioners who focus on mind, body and spirit and artisans who make handmade products. These vendors represent a unique, local community who promote healing on all levels.

We are pleased to support Imagine, a Westfield-based center for coping with grief and loss, by donating proceeds from the vendor registration fee.

Join our email list to stay in touch and learn about upcoming events and offerings from our vendors. For questions or comments contact us at njwellnesscollective@gmail.com.
